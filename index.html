This a friendly Sushi restaurant in the heart of the fish-market and was the original Sushizanmai restaurant (now a chain across the city). It has a great ambience and fast service. You are greeted by the chefs as you enter the restaurant. The food was delicious and of course extremely fresh given it is from the fish-market. We were there in the early evening and managed to get a table, but it was busy so you might want to reserve. We went to the fish market and looked around at the various sushi establishments and we liked this particular one, as it looked very authentic. I ordered an assortment of pieces (which is easier if you can't decide what to order). The staff were very friendly and I highly recommend this place.
The level of service seemed to vary around the place. We were lucky enough to sit at the sushi bar and watched the sushi chef prepare the meals, which was fascinating. He also introduced himself and was quite a jovial chap. However, the sushi itself was disappointing. I was still full from a curry bowl at lunch so I ordered the salad, which didn’t taste as good as it looked. It was an Interesting experience but not a great meal.
The wait for a table at around 2 PM on Saturday was reasonable and hostess that greeted us upfront was very friendly. We were traveling with a 5 month old therefore finding a restaurant that can fit a stroller is always a challenge. They were very accommodating with our situation as they made enough space for the stroller. Food was pretty decent. Fish was fresh but the presentation of the sushi could be a little better. I believe our expectations were too high since we were in Tsukiji market area. Overall a great experience with excellent customer service.
